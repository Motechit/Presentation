Meet the Counsellor

Charlotte Stone is a certified Psychotherapist, a Master's degree holder in Career Development from University of School and a Certified Career Counsellor by the Counsellor's Institute Board of the United States of Somewhere.

She has over 10 years experience helping variety of clients including students, recent graduates and experienced professionals. Her clients have a 98% success rate in their choosen fields including marketing, finance, technology, education among others.

She is a passionate listener, and skilled communicator; if you are looking for a career counsellor that will help discover your strengths, interests and a dream job, Charlotte Stone is the right person for you.

Contact her today to schedule a free consultation.




OurServices


Self-Discovery and Exploration

  • Self-assessment

    • We assist clients with self-assessment by facilitating discussions and exercises that uncover their strengths, interests, values, and skills, helping them gain clarity on their unique qualities and how they align with potential career paths.

  • Career exploration

    • We guide clients through career exploration by providing insights into various industries, job roles, and growth opportunities, enabling them to make informed decisions about potential career paths that resonate with their aspirations and strengths.

  • Goal setting

    • We support clients in goal setting by collaboratively defining clear and achievable short-term and long-term career objectives, and then devising actionable strategies to attain these goals, ensuring a structured and focused approach to their professional journey.

  • Skill enhancement

    • We aid clients in skill enhancement by identifying skill gaps, suggesting relevant courses and workshops, and providing a personalized plan for developing and refining the skills needed to excel in their chosen career path.

  • Transition support

    • We help clients smoothly navigate changes in their career journey, whether transitioning between industries, roles, or life stages, by offering guidance, resources, and strategies to make the transition as seamless and successful as possible.

Job Search and Professional Development

  • Resume and cover letter assistance

    • We provide valuable resume and cover letter assistance by offering expertise in crafting tailored and impactful documents that effectively showcase clients' skills, experiences, and accomplishments.

  • Interview preparation

    • We offer interview preparation by conducting mock interviews, providing constructive feedback, and sharing strategies that help clients build confidence, improve communication skills, and present themselves effectively.

  • Job search strategies

    • We support clients in developing effective job search strategies by offering guidance on utilizing online platforms, networking, targeting specific industries, tailoring applications, and maximizing their visibility.

  • Entrepreneurship insights

    • We provide valuable entrepreneurship insights by sharing knowledge about starting and managing businesses, market research, funding options, and practical steps to help clients navigate the complexities of entrepreneurship.

  • Negotiation skills

    • We assist clients in honing their negotiation skills by providing strategies and techniques to confidently negotiate job offers, promotions, salaries, benefits, and other workplace agreements.

Personal Branding and Work-Life Balance

  • Personal branding guidance

    • we offer personalized personal branding guidance to help clients develop a strong and authentic professional identity, providing advice on how to showcase their unique strengths, values, and expertise through their online presence, networking, and interactions.

  • Industry insights

    • We provide valuable industry insights by sharing up-to-date information about current trends, market demands, emerging opportunities, and challenges within specific industries,

  • Work-life balance strategies

    • We assist clients in achieving work-life balance by offering practical strategies to manage time, set boundaries, prioritize tasks, and maintain well-being

  • Support for challenges

    • We offer valuable support to clients facing challenges by providing a safe space to discuss and address issues such as career transitions, job dissatisfaction, workplace conflicts, and other obstacles

  • Retirement planning assistance

    • We provide retirement planning assistance by helping clients explore post-retirement options, whether it's pursuing part-time work, volunteering, starting a new venture, or engaging in hobbies
